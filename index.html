Amy Wibowo's online presence

A research report by u5351248 for ANU DESN1001 Semester 1 2017.

Amy Wibowo (@sailorhg) is a software engineer with interests in combining art and technology to make life better for people. In 2015 she launched BubbleSort Zines, a series of hand-drawn art and stories to explain computer science concepts to a target audience of high-school girls. She speaks regularly at tech conferences about her creative projects and experiences as a woman of colour in tech, and has also launched and designed several online projects, like Luna and Legoizer.

Wibowo uses many platforms to present and document her creations, and her larger projects also have their own online presence. Here are a few that this report that will be looking at:

  • Twitter (@sailorhg)
  • Instagram (@sailorhg)
  • Personal website (sailorhg.com)
  • Kickstarter (BubbleSort: Computer Science Zines and BubbleSort Zines 2.0: moar computer science zines!)
  • Medium (@sailorhg)
